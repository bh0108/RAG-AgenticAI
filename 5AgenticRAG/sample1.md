Acme Corporation – Remote Work Policy (2024)

1. Purpose
Acme Corporation supports flexible work arrangements that allow employees to perform their duties from home or other approved locations. This policy outlines expectations, security requirements, and communication standards for remote employees.

2. Eligibility
Employees are eligible for remote work if their role does not require daily on‑site presence. Managers must approve all remote work arrangements. Contractors may work remotely only with written authorization.

3. Work Hours and Availability
Remote employees must maintain the same core hours as on‑site staff: 9 AM to 4 PM Eastern Time. Employees are expected to be reachable via Microsoft Teams, email, or phone during these hours.

4. Security Requirements
All remote work must be performed using company‑issued devices. Employees must connect through the corporate VPN when accessing internal systems. Storing confidential data on personal devices is strictly prohibited.

5. Communication Expectations
Employees should attend all scheduled meetings with cameras on unless otherwise approved. Daily status updates must be posted in the team channel before 10 AM.

6. Equipment and Support
Acme provides laptops, headsets, and optional external monitors. Employees are responsible for maintaining a stable internet connection with at least 25 Mbps download speed.

7. Policy Violations
Failure to comply with this policy may result in revocation of remote work privileges or disciplinary action.
